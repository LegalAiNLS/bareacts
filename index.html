The Digital Bare Act Project

Bridging Engineering Precision with Legal Research

Founder's Note

Hi, I'm Rakshit Dwaram

As an engineer currently pursuing my LLB at NLSIU, I noticed a fundamental flaw in how we access our primary tools: Bare Acts. Most online sources are cluttered with ads, poorly formatted HTML, or outdated text.

I am applying my engineering background to build a clean, authoritative repository. This platform provides direct, indexed access to official PDFs, ensuring that the law you read is the law as it was printed.

The Engineering Approach

1. Verification

Every PDF is sourced directly from IndiaCode to ensure zero transcription errors.

2. Indexing

Metadata-driven search allows you to jump to specific sections within the PDF viewer.

3. Local Storage

Single-click download logic allows for offline reading during court sessions or exams.
